The deliver me project is a network of drop points that lets you send and deliver messages in a different and exciting way.

here's how to participate in the deliver me project

send a message:

Write a message, take an envelope, write someone’s name and address, and leave it at a deliver me drop point.

deliver a message:

Help deliver a message at a drop point by bringing it to its recipient, to a deliver me drop point closer to its destination, or to a trustworthy person who can take it there.

The deliver me project is a work in progress. Please voice any comments and suggestions here!

about the team

The deliver me project was created by Olin College of Engineering students Gaby Clarke, Marie-Caroline Finke, Aaron Greiner, Caz Nichols, and Cesar Santana in Spring 2015. The idea was inspired by a combination of Post Office Bay, a community mailbox that Aaron saw during his travels in the Galápagos Islands, and SOMEBODY, a messaging app that Gaby discovered at the Boston Museum of Fine Arts. We hope that you can use deliver me to bring adventure to your life.

You can reach us at contact@delivermeproject.com
